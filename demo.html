Light

Writes Upside-Down

The Zero Gravity Pen can be used to write in any orientation, including upside-down. It even works in outer space. NASA programs previously used pencils but because of the substantial dangers that broken pencil tips and graphite dust pose to electronics in zero gravity, the flammable nature of wood present in pencils, and the inadequate quality documentation produced by non-permanent or smeared record keeping, a better solution was needed.
